# Shop Mode

A list of 200 most common products offered by Deckard.

![Shop Interface](img/image-0004.png)

The Shop mode provides a streamlined e-commerce experience for ordering commonly required spare and wear parts directly from available stock. This B2B webshop is designed for quick identification and procurement of essential components, ensuring your machine uptime.

## Available Filter Options

### 1. Filter by Part Number

Enter complete or partial part number to narrow down the list of products. It's a predictive search field that helps you quickly find specific parts.

![Part Number Filter](img/image-0005.png)

### 2. Filter by Machine

Select a matching machine from the dropdown menu to see only compatible parts for your specific equipment.

![Machine Filter](img/image-0006.png)

### 3. View Toggle

Switch between grid view and list view to browse products in your preferred layout.

## Selecting Products

1. Click on a product in the list or grid view to display the product detail page
2. Adjust the quantity you want to add to the cart
3. Click "Add to cart" to add the product to your order

![Product Detail](img/image-0008.png)

## Shopping Cart

When you add product(s) to the cart, a sidebar appears displaying all the items in your cart.

![Cart Sidebar](img/image-0009.png)

### Available Options:

* **Back to product list** - Continue shopping
* **Save draft** - Saves the order in your Drafts folder so you can continue and finish later
* **Checkout** - Proceed to finalize your order

## Checkout Process

Click on checkout to go to the cart overview page. You can also access this page by clicking on the cart icon in the main navbar (1).

![Checkout Page](img/image-0010.png)

### Before Placing Your Order:

* Review all items in your cart
* Adjust quantities if needed
* Remove products you don't want to order
* **Important! Please enter "Internal reference number"!**

## Order Confirmation

After placing your order, a thank you screen appears with the following options:

1. **Back to dashboard** - Return to the main dashboard
2. **View active inquiries** - Go to the "My inquiries" section
3. **Call / E-mail** - Contact your area manager directly

---

**Tips for Efficient Shopping:**
* Use the predictive search to quickly find parts
* Filter by your specific machine for accurate results
* Save drafts if you need time to verify quantities
* Always include an internal reference number for tracking
