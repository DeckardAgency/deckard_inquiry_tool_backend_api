# Basic

## What is Textile Inquiry Tool?

![Inquiry Tool Dashboard](img/image-0001.png)

The Textile Inquiry Tool is a bespoke, state-of-the-art digital platform and Content Management System (CMS) meticulously designed to fundamentally transform how our global clientele interacts with Deckard regarding their machinery needs. It represents a strategic investment in enhancing customer service, operational efficiency, and ultimately, our competitive edge in the market.

At its core, this tool empowers users to seamlessly submit and manage their inquiries, provide critical information, and track the real-time status of their requests. Its design is singularly focused on significantly shortening response times – a key strategic objective – by streamlining communication and accelerating internal processes.

This sophisticated digital product is custom-tailored to meet Deckard's unique requirements, integrating several powerful functionalities:

### **Integrated Spare Parts Ordering (Webshop Logic):**

Facilitates direct, efficient ordering of commonly required genuine Deckard spare and wear parts from available stock. This functions as a dedicated B2B webshop, designed for quick identification and procurement of essential components, ensuring machine uptime.

### **Comprehensive Manual Inquiry System:**

Provides a robust mechanism for users to submit detailed inquiries for less common parts, custom requirements, or to report complex issues. Users can precisely describe their needs, specify machine types, input part names/numbers, provide elaborate descriptions, and crucially, upload supplementary images and files. This structured approach ensures our teams receive all necessary information upfront, reducing back-and-forth communication.

### **Seamless Communication & CRM Integration:**

Features a custom-built, secure communication system directly connected to Deckard's core CRM infrastructure. This ensures all client interactions, from initial inquiries to order updates and issue resolutions, are centralized, transparent, and immediately accessible to relevant teams. It significantly enhances internal collaboration and provides a unified customer view.

### **Centralized Client & Order History:**

Offers a comprehensive, easily accessible repository of Deckard clients' historical data, including all previous offers, orders, and associated communication. This provides our sales and service teams with invaluable context, enabling personalized service and informed decision-making.

### **Detailed Machine Management & Service Records:**

Maintains an accurate record of clients' Deckard machines, including installation dates and service history. This ensures precise part compatibility, proactive maintenance insights, and efficient support delivery based on the specific machine's lifecycle.

This digital product is engineered to deliver tangible business benefits by:

- Enhancing User Engagement and Satisfaction: By providing a transparent, intuitive, and self-service platform for all inquiry and ordering needs.
- Improving Response Times: Drastically cutting down the time from inquiry submission to resolution or fulfillment, directly impacting customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.
- Streamlining Operational Processes: Automating data capture and routing, reducing manual effort, and enabling faster internal processing of requests.
- Supporting Upsale and Cross-Sale Opportunities: By having a consolidated view of client history and machine data, our sales and service teams are better positioned to identify and act on opportunities to offer relevant products and services.
- Providing Data-Driven Insights: Centralizing interactions and order data provides valuable analytics for identifying trends, optimizing inventory, and refining service strategies.

In essence, the Textile Inquiry Tool is not just an application; it is a strategic platform for optimized customer interaction and operational excellence, designed to drive efficiency, enhance service delivery, and support Deckard's growth objectives.

## Understanding User Interface (UI)

![User interface overview](img/image-0002.png)

The design of our application's User Interface (UI) is meticulously crafted to ensure efficiency, intuitive navigation, and a seamless user experience for all stakeholders, from frontline staff to regional managers. A well-designed UI is not merely about aesthetics; it is a critical component for maximizing productivity, reducing training overheads, and ensuring rapid access to vital information.

The application's UI is systematically structured around the following core elements, each serving a distinct purpose in facilitating user interaction and information flow:

### **1. Navigation Bar (Header)**

Positioned prominently at the top of the application, serves as the primary control panel for the entire system. Its strategic placement ensures consistent access to essential functionalities regardless of the user's current location within the application.

### **2. Global Search Functionality**

Integrated within the Navigation Bar, the Global Search is a powerful tool designed for rapid information retrieval across the entire application ecosystem. It offers intuitive search capabilities, often with predictive text and intelligent filtering, to help users quickly locate machines, parts, orders, inquiries, and other relevant data.

### **3. Sidebar / Main Menu**

Located on the left side of the application frame, functions as the comprehensive main menu. It organizes the application's various modules and features into logical groups, providing structured access to different areas of functionality (e.g., Shop, Manual Inquiry, Order History, Machine Hub).

### **4. Application Frame (Content Area)**

The Application Frame, or content area, is the dynamic workspace where all user interactions, data displays, and operational tasks take place. This is where forms are filled, lists are presented, and specific feature functionalities are executed.
