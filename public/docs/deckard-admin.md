# Administrator Panel

## Home Dashboard

The Administrator Home Dashboard provides comprehensive oversight of all system activity and performance metrics.

### Performance Overview
Monitor key business metrics with customizable date range filtering:
- **Number of Orders**: Total order count for the selected period
- **Number of Inquiries**: Total inquiry volume
- **Active Orders**: Currently processing orders
- **Cancelled Orders**: Orders terminated before completion
- **Active Carts**: Shopping carts currently in use
- **Completed Carts**: Successfully checked-out carts
- **Total Shop Revenue**: Complete revenue generated
- **Cancelled Orders Revenue**: Potential revenue lost to cancellations

Use the date range selector to analyze performance across different time periods and identify trends.

### Active Orders and Inquiries
Quick-access view of all current orders and inquiries requiring attention. This real-time overview helps prioritize urgent items and maintain smooth operations.

### Order History
Browse all historical orders organized into three categories:
- **Latest**: Most recently placed orders
- **Completed**: Successfully fulfilled orders
- **Cancelled**: Orders that were cancelled

## Customer Hub

### Accounts List
Manage all customer accounts with full administrative control:
- **View**: Complete list of all active customer accounts
- **Add**: Create new customer accounts
- **Edit**: Modify existing account information
- **Delete**: Remove accounts that are no longer needed

### Account Detail View
Access comprehensive account information organized into sections:

#### Basic Information
Core account details and primary contact data

#### Contact Information
Complete contact details for account communication

#### Associated Contacts
Overview of all contacts linked to the account, enabling multi-user access management

#### Addresses Overview
List of all shipping and billing addresses associated with the account

#### Orders Overview
Complete order history for the specific account, useful for tracking customer activity and relationship management

#### Machines List
Inventory of all machines installed at the customer's location

## Shop Orders

### Overview
Complete management interface for all customer-placed shop orders.

### Order Organization
Orders are grouped into three categories for efficient management:
- **Latest**: Most recent orders
- **Completed**: Successfully fulfilled orders
- **Cancelled**: Terminated orders

### Management Actions
- **Edit**: Modify order details and information
- **Delete**: Remove orders from the system

### Order Full View
Comprehensive order details including:
- **Items Overview**: Complete list of all products in the order
- **Account Details**: Customer account information
- **Contact Information**: Order-specific contact details
- **Order Status**: Current processing stage
- **Shipping Address**: Delivery destination
- **Delivery Type**: Selected shipping method
- **Pricing**: Itemized costs, taxes, and totals

---

## Manual Orders

Manual orders function identically to shop orders but are created directly by administrators rather than through customer self-service. All features, views, and management options mirror the Shop Orders module.

## Discounts

### Discount Management
Create and manage promotional pricing campaigns:

#### Discount Configuration
- **Custom Name**: Identify discount campaigns
- **Date Range**: Set "from" and "to" dates for time-limited promotions
- **Discount Amount**: Define percentage or fixed-amount reductions
- **Product Assignment**: Group multiple products under single discount campaigns

#### Actions
- **Add**: Create new discount campaigns
- **Edit**: Modify existing discounts
- **Delete**: Remove expired or unused discount groups

## E-commerce Settings

### Country List
Manage shipping destinations and logistics zones:
- View all available countries with assigned DHL zones
- **Add**: Enable shipping to new countries
- **Edit**: Modify country settings and zone assignments
- **Delete**: Remove countries from available destinations

### Tax Types List
Configure tax rules for different jurisdictions:
- Manage country-specific tax regulations
- **Add**: Create new tax types
- **Edit**: Update tax rates and rules
- **Delete**: Remove obsolete tax configurations

### Payment Types
Define available payment methods for customers:
- **Add/Edit/Delete**: Manage payment options
- **Short Description**: Brief payment method overview
- **Details**: Comprehensive payment information
- **Documentation**: Upload terms, conditions, or setup guides

### Delivery Types
Configure shipping and delivery options:
- **Add/Edit/Delete**: Manage delivery methods
- **Short Description**: Quick delivery option overview
- **Documentation**: Upload delivery terms, timeframes, or special instructions

### Warehouse List
Manage fulfillment center information:

#### Warehouse Details
- Contact person
- Warehouse name
- Complete address and city
- Phone number
- Email address
- Website URL
- Short description
- Related documentation

**Actions**: Add, edit, or delete warehouse locations

### Delivery Prices
Zone-based shipping cost management:
- Configure pricing for different geographic zones

- **Edit**: Adjust zone rates
- **Delete**: Remove unused pricing zones

## Fuel Surcharge

Manage fuel-related shipping cost adjustments:
- Configure different surcharge rates for various shipping options
- Each shipping method can have unique fuel surcharge pricing
- **Add**: Create new surcharge options
- **Edit**: Update surcharge rates
- **Delete**: Remove obsolete surcharge configurations

> **Note:** Fuel surcharges help maintain accurate shipping costs during periods of fuel price volatility.

---

## Packaging Price

Size-based packaging cost management:
- Configure pricing based on item dimensions
- Set base prices for different package sizes
- **Add**: Create new packaging price tiers
- **Edit**: Adjust pricing for existing sizes
- **Delete**: Remove unused pricing options

This ensures accurate cost calculation based on actual packaging requirements for each order.
