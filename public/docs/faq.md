# Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Deckard Inquiry Tool.

## General Questions

### What is the purpose of this tool?

The Inquiry Tool allows you to request spare parts for your Deckard machines efficiently. You can browse parts, create custom requests, and track the status of your inquiries.

### Who can use this tool?

The tool is available to registered Deckard customers with active accounts. Contact your administrator for access.

### How do I get support?

For technical support or questions about the tool, please contact your Deckard representative or submit a support ticket through the system.

## Creating Inquiries

### What's the difference between Shop Mode and Manual Entry?

**Shop Mode** lets you browse the parts catalog and add items to your cart like an online shop. It's best when you know the exact parts you need.

**Manual Entry** allows you to describe parts without catalog numbers. Use this when you need custom or unlisted parts.

### Can I save an inquiry as a draft?

Yes! You can save your inquiry at any time and return to complete it later. Find your drafts under **My Inquiries > Drafts**.

### How many files can I attach?

You can attach multiple files (images, PDFs, documents) up to 5MB each. Supported formats include JPG, PNG, WEBP, PDF, and DOC.

### Can I edit an inquiry after submission?

Once submitted, inquiries cannot be edited. However, you can create a new inquiry or contact support for assistance with modifications.

## Account & Access

### How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen and follow the instructions sent to your email.

### Can I switch between user accounts?

Yes, use the "Switch User" option in the sidebar menu if you have access to multiple accounts.

### How do I update my company information?

Administrators can update company details in the **My Company** section. Regular users should contact their administrator.

## Machine Management

### How do I add a new machine?

Navigate to **All Machines** and use the "Add Machine" button. You'll need your machine's serial number and model information.

### What if my machine isn't listed?

Select "Other/Unlisted" when creating your inquiry and provide the machine details manually. Our team will assist with identification.

### Can I delete a machine?

Only administrators can remove machines from your company's inventory. Contact your admin for machine management.

## Technical Issues

### The page isn't loading. What should I do?

Try refreshing your browser or clearing your cache. If the issue persists, contact technical support.

### My files won't upload. Why?

Check that:
* Files are under 5MB each
* File formats are supported (JPG, PNG, WEBP, PDF, DOC)
* You have a stable internet connection

### I see an error message. What does it mean?

Error messages provide specific information about the issue. If you're unsure, take a screenshot and contact support.
